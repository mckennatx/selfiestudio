Selfie Studio
The app that rates your selfie bae, basic, or ratchet.
Tell Me More

Description

The average person will take 5-6 selfies before posting it to social media. Selfie Studio will tell you if your selfie is worth sharing with 1000+ of your closest friends.

BAE

If Selfie Studio thinks you look great, you'll get a "bae" rating, and you are recommended to share that picture immediately!

BASIC

If Selfie Studio thinks you look aight, you'll get a "basic" rating. It's not the best picture you've taken, but it's still worth sharing on social media!

RATCHET

If Selfie Studio thinks you look like you just woke up from a rough night on 6th street, you'll get a "ratchet" rating, and will be strongly encourged to not share it to social media.

Features

Share your pictures to Facebook.

Opens front-facing camera automatically.

Upload a photo from picture gallery.

Automatically save selfie to your photos.

The ability to change personal settings.

Image comparison algorithm.
